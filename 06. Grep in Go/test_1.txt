Artificial intelligence and machine learning are increasingly integrated into DevOps to automate tasks and enhance predictive analytics.
Kubernetes has become the standard for container orchestration, facilitating efficient management of containerized applications.
The adoption of DevSecOps emphasizes incorporating security measures throughout the DevOps pipeline.
Infrastructure as Code (IaC) allows teams to manage and provision infrastructure through code, enhancing consistency and automation.
The rise of serverless computing enables developers to focus on code without managing underlying infrastructure, streamlining deployment processes.
